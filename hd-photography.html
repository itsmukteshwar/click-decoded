Our Process

Brief to Delivered Gallery
in 5 Business Days.

A structured workflow that keeps every shoot on-brief, on-time, and on-brand — with zero surprises at delivery.

01

Brief & Mood Board

We gather your brand guidelines, reference images, and key objectives — then produce a detailed shot list and mood board for your approval before anything is booked or scheduled.

02

Location & Logistics

Studio or on-location — we handle venue scouting, equipment logistics, lighting setup, prop sourcing, and model coordination if required. You show up; we handle everything else.

03

The Shoot

Professional photographer and art director on set for the full duration. Tethered shooting means you can review images live on a monitor — ensuring sign-off on key frames before the kit is packed away.

04

Retouch & Deliver

Selects edited, colour-graded, and retouched within 5 business days. Delivered in organised folders by usage type — web, print, social — with full naming conventions for easy file management.

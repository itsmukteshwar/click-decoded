## The B2B SEO Landscape in 2025

Search has changed dramatically. AI-generated answers, Google's SGE, and zero-click results mean your old playbook needs updating.

Here's what actually moves the needle for B2B businesses in 2025.

## 1. Technical Foundation First

Before content, fix your technical SEO:

- **Core Web Vitals** — LCP under 2.5s, CLS under 0.1
- **Mobile-first indexing** — test on real devices
- **Schema markup** — Organization, Service, FAQ, Breadcrumb
- **Internal linking** — spread link equity to key service pages

## 2. Content That Answers Real Questions

B2B buyers research heavily before contacting you. Your content should answer:

- "What does [service] cost in India?"
- "How long does SEO take to work?"
- "Which agency should I hire for Google Ads?"

Target **bottom-of-funnel** keywords first — they convert.

## 3. Local SEO for Indian B2B

If you serve specific cities, local SEO is non-negotiable:

- Fully optimized Google Business Profile
- City-specific service landing pages
- Local citations on Indian directories

## 4. AI Search Optimization (GEO)

Generative engines (ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity) are now sourcing content. To get cited:

- Write authoritative, factual long-form content
- Include statistics, case studies, and named experts
- Use clear headings — AI scanners love structure

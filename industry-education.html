Challenges We Solve

We've worked with enough education businesses to know exactly what's blocking student acquisition.

🔍

Low Search Visibility

Parents Google "best school in Bhopal" or "NEET coaching near me" before visiting. If you're not on page 1, you're losing admissions to competitors who are — every single day.

📋

No Structured Lead Capture

Most institutions have a "Contact Us" form going to an email nobody checks promptly. We build WhatsApp-first enquiry flows that capture, qualify, and respond to leads in real time.

📅

Admission Season Unpreparedness

Admission season is 3–4 months. Paid campaigns must be planned 60 days in advance or you waste budget at peak. We build your admission calendar proactively, not reactively.

🌐

Weak Website That Loses Trust

Slow, outdated websites with no fee structure, no results page, and no faculty profiles lose parents in the first 5 seconds. We rebuild trust through design and clear information architecture.

📱

Zero Meta & YouTube Presence

Parents research institutions on Facebook and YouTube — yet most education brands ignore these channels. That's a low-cost opportunity for early movers with well-placed video and social ads.

🤖

No Post-Enquiry Nurture

Most institutions don't follow up systematically. WhatsApp sequences that send fee details, open day invites, results proofs, and parent testimonials convert more enquiries into paid admissions.

We Plan Around Your Admission Windows

Education marketing is seasonal. Most institutions start too late. We start 60 days before you need results.

🌸 Primary Season

March – June

The peak admission window for schools, coaching institutes, and colleges. Highest search volume, highest competition — your campaigns must be live by February.

  • Landing pages live by February end
  • Google Ads + Meta campaigns running from March 1
  • WhatsApp enquiry sequences automated and tested
  • GMB optimised for "near me" searches
🍂 Secondary Season

November – January

The second admission window — often overlooked. JEE/NEET droppers, lateral entry admissions, and early next-year registrations all happen here at lower ad costs.

  • Lower CPL due to reduced competition
  • Retargeting past-season enquiries who didn't convert
  • Early bird fee campaigns via WhatsApp broadcast
  • Content push for results and rankings pages

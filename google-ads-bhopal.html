📋 How It Works

Our Process — Step by Step

Transparent process. You know what we're doing at every stage.

01
Account Audit / Setup
We audit your existing Google Ads account for wasted spend, poor Quality Scores, and missing conversions. For new clients, we build the account from scratch with proper structure.
02
Keyword & Competitor Research
We research every search term your Bhopal customers use, identify competitor ad strategies, and build a keyword plan that targets maximum-intent searches while eliminating irrelevant traffic.
03
Campaign Build & Ad Copy
We structure your campaigns correctly (Search, Display, Remarketing), write compelling ad copy with strong CTAs, and set up conversion tracking so every lead is attributed accurately.
04
Launch & First 2-Week Optimisation
Post-launch, we monitor closely — pausing poor performers, scaling winners, adjusting bids, and expanding negative keyword lists. The first 2 weeks are critical for performance signals.
05
Monthly Reporting & Scaling
Every month: a full report on spend, conversions, CPA, and ROAS. We scale budgets to campaigns and ad groups that demonstrate strong ROI and continuously test new ad copy and audiences.

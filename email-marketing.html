Our Process

Audit to First Campaign
in 2 Weeks.

01

Audit & ESP Setup

We audit your existing email setup — deliverability health, existing sequences, list segmentation, and template quality. ESP configured (or migrated), domain authentication set up, and sender reputation assessed.

02

List Clean & Segment

Full list hygiene pass — removing invalid addresses, hard bounces, and spam traps. Remaining contacts segmented by engagement level, purchase history, and funnel stage for targeted sending from Day 1.

03

Build & Launch

Welcome sequence and first campaign written, designed, and tested across 40+ email clients. A/B subject line variants set up. Campaign scheduled and launched with optimal send time based on your audience data.

Monthly performance review — what subject lines won, which segments engaged, which automations converted. Data used to plan the next month's calendar, refine copy angles, and expand automation coverage.

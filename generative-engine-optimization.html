What is GEO?

Generative Engine Optimization — The New SEO

Traditional SEO puts you in the blue links. GEO puts you inside the answer. When AI engines like ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity generate a response to a user query, they pull from trusted, well-structured, authoritative sources — and GEO is the discipline of making your brand one of them.

GEO combines entity optimization, topical authority building, structured content creation, and citation-signal engineering to ensure that when someone asks an AI about your industry, product, or service — your brand shows up in the answer.

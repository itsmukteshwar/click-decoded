Templates (called HSM — Highly Structured Messages) are pre-approved message formats required for all outbound business-initiated WhatsApp messages. Meta reviews them to prevent spam. Our team writes templates that comply with Meta's content policies and pass approval. Once approved (typically 24–48 hours), they can be sent at scale. Template approval is a one-time process per template — approved templates can be reused indefinitely.

The WhatsApp green tick (Official Business Account badge) verifies that your WhatsApp number is linked to a legitimate, Meta-verified business. It builds trust with recipients and reduces opt-out rates. We manage the application process as part of setup — it requires your business to be verified on Meta Business Manager. Approval timelines vary (2–8 weeks) and are ultimately decided by Meta, but our application preparation significantly improves success rates.

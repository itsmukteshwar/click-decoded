📋 How It Works

Our Process — Step by Step

Transparent process. You know what we're doing at every stage.

01
Discovery & Requirement Gathering
We meet (in-person at our Bhopal office or virtually) to understand your business, target audience, competitors, and goals. We document requirements and agree on the scope, timeline, and payment terms.
02
Wireframes & Design Mockups
We create wireframes for the key pages, then move to high-fidelity design mockups. You review and approve designs before any development begins — no surprises.
03
Development & CMS Setup
We build your website on the agreed tech stack (custom HTML/CSS, WordPress, or Next.js). We set up the CMS so your team can update content without touching code.
04
Testing & QA
We test across browsers (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge), devices (mobile, tablet, desktop), and run performance audits. We fix every issue before handing over to you.
05
Launch & Handover
We deploy to your hosting, configure DNS, set up Google Analytics and Search Console, and submit your sitemap to Google. We train your team on the CMS and hand over all access credentials.

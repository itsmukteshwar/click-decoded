43% of the Web Runs on WordPress. There's a Good Reason.

WordPress is the world's most widely used CMS — not because it's the easiest, but because it's the most flexible. Properly built, a WordPress site can serve a 5-page business brochure or a 10,000-product e-commerce store with equal reliability.

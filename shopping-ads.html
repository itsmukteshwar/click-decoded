Shopping Ad Formats
We Master.

Google's Shopping ecosystem has evolved well beyond basic product listings. We use every format strategically.

🛒
Core

Standard Shopping

Classic product listing ads showing image, title, price, and store. The highest-intent ad format on Google — users see exactly what they're getting before clicking.

⚡
AI-Powered

Performance Max

Google's AI-driven campaign type that shows your products across Search, Shopping, Display, YouTube, and Gmail — maximising conversion value from one campaign.

🔄
Retargeting

Dynamic Remarketing

Show the exact products viewed by past visitors across the Google Display Network — highly personalised ads that pull warm traffic back to complete their purchase.

🏪
Local

Local Inventory Ads

Show in-store product availability to nearby shoppers searching on Google. Drives foot traffic by displaying real-time stock levels and store location.

📱
Mobile

Showcase Shopping

Lifestyle image ads that appear for broad, upper-funnel queries — introducing your brand and product range to shoppers still in the discovery phase.

🗺️
Maps

Shopping on Google Maps

Appear when shoppers search for products "near me" on Google Maps — critical for retailers with physical stores looking to drive walk-in customers.

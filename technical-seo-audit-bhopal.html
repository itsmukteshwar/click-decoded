Our Process — Step by Step

Clear steps, no black boxes. You know what we're doing and why at every stage.

01
Discovery & Access Setup
You give us access to Google Search Console and Google Analytics. We connect both, review your current performance data, and understand your business goals and target Bhopal keywords.
02
Automated Crawl + Manual Review
We run a deep crawl of your entire site and layer manual review on top — automated tools miss things human auditors catch. We cross-reference crawl data with your GSC index coverage report.

You receive a detailed PDF report with every issue ranked by impact — Critical, High, Medium, and Low. We walk you through it on a call and give you a clear implementation roadmap.

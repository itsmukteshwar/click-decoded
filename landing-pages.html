The Single Biggest Waste in Digital Advertising

You're Sending Ad Traffic to Your Homepage. That's Costing You 60% of Your Conversions.

Your homepage is designed for everyone. A landing page is designed for one person, one offer, one action. When a visitor lands on your homepage from a Google Ad for "interior designers in Bhopal", they see your full navigation, services list, about section, and blog — and most of them leave without converting.

A dedicated landing page removes every distraction — no nav, no links away, one CTA, message-matched to the ad. Studies consistently show landing pages convert 2–5× better than homepages for the same traffic.

✓ No navigation — visitors can only take one action
✓ Message match — headline mirrors your ad copy
✓ Single CTA — one goal, one button, nothing else
✓ A/B tested — data drives every design decision

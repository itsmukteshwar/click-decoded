📋 How It Works

Our Process — Step by Step

Transparent process. You know what we're doing at every stage.

01
WhatsApp Business API Setup
We set up your official WhatsApp Business API account via an approved provider (Interakt, Wati, or direct Meta). This unlocks automation, broadcasts, and chatbots — unavailable on the regular WhatsApp Business app.
02
Workflow Design & Mapping
We map your customer journey — from first contact to post-sale — and design automation flows for each stage. You approve every message template before submission to Meta.
03
Template Approval & Bot Build
We submit message templates to Meta for approval (typically 24–48 hrs). Simultaneously, we build the chatbot flow and connect integrations (CRM, forms, e-commerce).
04
Testing & Go-Live
We test every automation flow end-to-end — lead submission → WhatsApp reply, order → confirmation message, and bot conversation paths. After sign-off, we go live.
05
Training & Monitoring
We train your Bhopal team on the dashboard — viewing conversations, handling escalations, and sending broadcasts. We monitor the first 2 weeks closely and fix any edge cases.

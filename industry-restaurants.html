Challenges We Solve

We've worked with enough businesses in this industry to know exactly what's holding you back.

📍

Not Appearing in 'Near Me' Searches

'Restaurants near me' is one of Google's most searched phrases. If your GMB profile isn't optimised, you don't appear — and the 3 restaurants that do appear get all the footfall.

📸

Unappetising Photo Presentation

Food photography is the single biggest driver of online restaurant discovery. A well-shot dish photo on Instagram or Google can drive 10x more clicks than text descriptions.

⭐

Low Review Count

A restaurant with 200 reviews and 4.5 stars outperforms one with 20 reviews and 4.8 stars — because volume signals credibility. We build systematic review generation.

📱

No Social Media Presence

Instagram and Facebook are where food discovery happens. Without a consistent posting strategy with professional photos, you're invisible to the 18–35 audience that eats out most.

🤖

No Table Reservation Automation

A WhatsApp bot that confirms table reservations, sends reminders, and follows up with feedback requests improves guest experience and reduces no-shows.

🌐

Aggregator Dependency

Swiggy and Zomato take 25–30% of every order. A direct ordering website or WhatsApp ordering system captures the same order at full margin.

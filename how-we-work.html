The Process

Six Steps From First Call to Real Results

1
Discovery

☎️ The First Conversation

We start with a 30-minute strategy call — no sales pitch. We want to understand your business, your current digital situation, what's been tried, what hasn't worked, and what success actually looks like for you. It's a conversation, not a presentation.

✓
We listen more than we talk
✓
We ask about your goals, not just your budget
✓
If we're not the right fit, we say so here — not 3 months later
📅 Day 1⏱️ 30 min💬 Phone or Video
2
Audit & Research

🔍 We Do Our Homework First

Before we recommend anything, we dig in. SEO clients get a technical audit. Ads clients get a current-campaign teardown. Web clients get a performance review. We look at your competitors, your market, and your existing data — then build a real picture of the opportunity.

✓
Technical SEO audit (site crawl, GSC data, Core Web Vitals)
✓
Competitor gap analysis — what they rank for that you don't
✓
Current ads performance review (if applicable)
✓
Keyword opportunity mapping
📅 Day 2–3📊 Data-led
3
Strategy & Proposal

📋 A Plan That Makes Sense

We present a clear strategy document — not a generic "we'll do SEO for you" proposal. You'll see exactly what we plan to do, in what order, why, and what we expect it to accomplish. Pricing is itemised. Timelines are realistic. No smoke, no mirrors.

✓
Written strategy with clear deliverables and timelines
✓
Honest projection of what to expect and when
✓
Itemised pricing — you know what you're paying for
✓
Questions welcome — we revise until it's right
📅 Day 4–5📝 Written proposal
4
Onboarding & Setup

⚙️ Access, Setup, and Alignment

Once you're in, we get to work quickly. We'll request access to what we need (GSC, GA4, Ads accounts, hosting, etc.), set up our tracking and reporting infrastructure, and align on communication rhythm. Most clients are fully onboarded within the first week.

✓
Dedicated point of contact assigned — one person, always reachable
✓
Tracking and reporting dashboard set up
✓
Access to tools requested — minimal friction
✓
Communication cadence agreed — WhatsApp, email, or call
📅 Day 6–7🤝 Kick-off
5
Execution

🚀 The Work Actually Starts Here

We execute the strategy — methodically, not frantically. Every task is logged. Every deliverable is tracked. You won't hear silence for weeks and then get a vague update. If something shifts or we find a better opportunity, we tell you immediately.

✓
Weekly progress updates — brief, to the point
✓
Deliverables shared on agreed schedule
✓
Any strategic pivots communicated proactively
✓
You can ask for a status update any time — we respond same day
📅 Week 2 onwards📊 Tracked weekly
6
Reporting & Growth

📈 Monthly Reviews. No Vanity Metrics.

Every month, you get a clear report — the numbers that matter, not a wall of screenshots designed to impress. We review what worked, what didn't, and what we're adjusting. We treat monthly reviews as a strategy session, not a formality.

✓
Clear monthly report — written in plain language
✓
KPIs reviewed vs targets set at the start
✓
Honest assessment — what's working, what needs adjustment
✓
Plan for the next month discussed and documented
📅 Monthly📋 Written report🔄 Continuous
Our Promise

Things You'll Never Have to Deal With

🚫 Ghosting after onboarding

We stay reachable throughout the engagement. One dedicated contact, same-day responses, proactive communication — always.

🚫 Reports full of fluff

No impressions graphs, no "brand awareness" metrics used to hide a bad month. You get the numbers that actually matter to your business.

🚫 Surprise charges

What we quote is what you pay. Any scope change is discussed before it happens — not billed after the fact.

🚫 Work done by juniors, signed off by seniors

Your account is handled by the specialist who pitched it — not handed off to a trainee once you sign.

🚫 Black hat tactics that risk your site

Zero paid links from link farms, zero keyword stuffing, zero cloaking. We've had zero Google penalties in 12+ years.

🚫 Being told everything is "going great"

If something isn't performing, we tell you early and honestly — and we bring a plan with that news, not just the problem.

Our Process

Ship Your Products.
Receive Perfect Images.

A streamlined intake-to-delivery workflow built for volume — no location visit required from your side for standard product shoots.

01

SKU List & Brief

Share your product list, target marketplaces, required angles, and any styling notes. We generate a confirmed shot list and quote within 24 hours — no ambiguity, no scope creep.

02

Ship or Drop Off

Ship products to our studio or drop them off in Indore. We handle intake, cataloguing, and product care throughout the shoot. Products are returned insured and in original condition.

03

Shoot & Retouch

Products shot in batches by category for lighting efficiency. Each SKU receives full retouching — background removal, colour correction, and blemish clean-up — before being flagged for quality check.

04

Deliver & Upload

Finals delivered via Google Drive organised by SKU, angle, and platform format. We can also upload directly to your Shopify, WooCommerce, or Amazon Seller Central account to save your team time.

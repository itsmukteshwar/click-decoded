Our Process

Brief to Published Content
in 10–14 Days.

A structured campaign workflow that eliminates the chaos typically associated with influencer marketing — clear timelines, managed communications, on-brief content every time.

01

Campaign Brief

We capture your brand objectives, target audience, key message, budget, and preferred creator aesthetic. A campaign strategy deck is presented within 48 hours with recommended creator tiers and platform mix.

02

Creator Matching

Shortlist of 8–15 vetted creators presented with audience analytics, engagement data, past brand work, and our team's qualitative assessment — you approve the final selection before any outreach begins.

03

Brief, Review & Approve

Creator briefs issued, content drafts collected and reviewed by our team, client approval sought on final content before publishing. Revision cycles handled by us — zero direct creator management on your side.

04

Publish & Report

Content goes live on agreed dates. Post-campaign performance data collected at 7 and 30 days. Full report delivered covering views, engagement, link clicks, and campaign ROAS with recommendations for future rounds.

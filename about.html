Our Story

From a Bhopal Startup to India's AI-Ready Growth Agency

Click Decoded began in 2014 as a lean web and SEO shop in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh — operating under the registered entity Aharnish Infotech Private Limited. Back then, we helped local businesses get found on Google. We were scrappy, client-obsessed, and relentlessly result-focused.

Over the next decade, we grew into a full-service digital agency — adding PPC, Meta Ads, e-commerce development, and content. We served clients across real estate, healthcare, education, manufacturing, and B2B SaaS. The one constant across every engagement: we treated every client's budget as if it were our own.

In 2023, we made a deliberate pivot. AI wasn't a buzzword for us — it was the next wave of leverage. We retrained our team, rebuilt our internal processes using n8n and GPT-4o, and launched a dedicated AI Automation practice. Today, Click Decoded is one of the few Indian agencies that combines genuine digital marketing expertise with real, working AI implementations.

Legally registered as Aharnish Infotech Private Limited (CIN: U72900MP2014PTC032149), incorporated in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh. Click Decoded is the agency's primary brand through which all digital services, white-label partnerships, and AI automation projects are delivered.

FAQ

Answer Engine Optimization — Common Questions

Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is the practice of structuring and optimizing your content so it gets selected by search engines and AI systems to appear in zero-click answer formats — including Google's featured snippets, AI Overviews, People Also Ask boxes, and voice search responses. AEO focuses on providing clear, direct, authoritative answers that AI and search engines can present directly to users without requiring a click.
Traditional SEO aims to rank your page in the top 10 results, requiring users to click through to your site. AEO aims to make your content the direct answer that appears before any click is needed — in featured snippets, AI Overviews, or voice responses. AEO requires specific content formatting (Q&A structure, concise definitions, step-by-step guides) alongside standard SEO signals like domain authority and backlinks.
AEO covers all major zero-click formats: featured snippets (paragraph, list, and table formats), Google AI Overviews, People Also Ask (PAA) boxes, voice search responses on Google Assistant, Siri, and Alexa, and conversational AI answers in Gemini and ChatGPT browse mode. Each format requires slightly different optimization — we handle all of them.
Featured snippet wins typically appear within 2–6 weeks of content restructuring and schema deployment. Google AI Overview improvements usually show within 4–8 weeks. People Also Ask positions often shift within 3–5 weeks. Voice search optimization results correlate with featured snippet wins and follow a similar timeline.
AEO relies on several JSON-LD schema types: FAQPage for question-and-answer content, HowTo for step-by-step guide content, Speakable for voice search optimization, Article and NewsArticle for editorial content, and QAPage for community Q&A-style pages. We implement all relevant schema types based on your content and target answer format.

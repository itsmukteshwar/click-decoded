6-Month Learning Journey

Structured growth, real milestones, and a mentor by your side every step.

1

Learn tools, get assigned to your track mentor, shadow live projects, and complete your first mini-task on a real client account.

2

Month 2 — First Independent Deliverable

Take ownership of specific tasks within client accounts. Run your first fully independent deliverable with mentor review and feedback.

3

Month 3 — Go Deeper

Move from task execution to strategy input. Contribute to client planning, keyword research, campaign direction, or automation architecture.

4

Month 4 — Own a Full Project

Lead a mini project end-to-end — from brief to delivery. Mid-program review with your mentor to assess growth and adjust goals.

5

Month 5 — Advanced Execution

Handle higher-complexity tasks independently. Build your portfolio case study from work done on real accounts — results, process, learnings.

✓

Month 6 — Completion & PPO Review

Final performance review, certificate, LinkedIn recommendation from your mentor, and PPO discussion if applicable. You leave job-ready.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a stipend?

No — and we're upfront about it. Instead, we invest ₹20K–₹40K/month in premium tools, senior mentor hours, real client exposure, and certification support. The value you take away is worth far more than a small monthly payment.

Do I need a degree or prior experience?

Neither. School dropout, college dropout, fresher, career switcher — we don't care about your paper trail. Show us your passion and willingness to learn, and you have a real shot.

Can I intern remotely?

First month is in-office at Bhopal for onboarding. After that, hybrid options are available by track.

Will I get a certificate?

Yes — a formal completion certificate and a LinkedIn recommendation letter from your mentor.

What are my chances of a full-time offer?

Real — we've converted multiple interns to full-time roles. It depends on performance and openings at the time.

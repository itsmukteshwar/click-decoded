Our Process

From Brief to Live Chatbot in 4 Steps

1

Knowledge Gathering

We collect your FAQs, product info, pricing, policies, and tone guidelines. This becomes the chatbot's training knowledge base — everything it knows comes from your content.

2

Personality & Flow Design

We define the bot's persona, conversation flows, escalation triggers, and fallback responses — then share a flowchart for your approval before building anything.

3

Build, Train & Test

We build the chatbot, train it on your knowledge base, run 50+ test conversations covering edge cases, and refine responses until accuracy is above 90%.

4

Launch & Optimise

We go live, monitor the first 500 conversations, fix any gaps, and set up monthly reports on top queries, unresolved cases, and conversion performance.

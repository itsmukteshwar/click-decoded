📋 How It Works

Our Process — Step by Step

Transparent process. You know what we're doing at every stage.

01
Account Audit / Fresh Setup
We audit your existing Meta Business Manager for pixel health, audience quality, and campaign structure. For new clients, we set up the Business Manager, pixel, conversion events, and catalogue from scratch.
02
Audience & Creative Strategy
We define your target Bhopal audience segments (cold, warm, retargeting) and build a creative brief for each funnel stage — awareness, consideration, and conversion.
03
Campaign Build & Launch
We build campaigns with proper campaign objectives, ad set targeting, and creative assets. We set up A/B tests for audiences and creatives from day one.
04
First 2-Week Optimisation
We monitor delivery, kill underperforming ad sets, and scale winners. The first 2 weeks establish the algorithm's performance baseline.
05
Monthly Reports & Scaling
Monthly report covering spend, impressions, CPL/CPA, ROAS, and creative performance. We refresh creatives every 4–6 weeks to prevent audience fatigue.

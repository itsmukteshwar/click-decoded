When you visit our website, our servers and third-party analytics tools may automatically collect:

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Marketing (with consent) — to send newsletters or service updates if you opt in

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies. A cookie is a small text file stored on your device when you visit a website. We use:

  • Essential cookies — required for the website to function correctly
  • Analytics cookies — to understand website traffic and usage patterns (via Google Analytics)
  • Marketing cookies — to measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns (Google Ads, Meta Pixel)

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website. For full details, see our Cookie Policy.

6. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers within India and/or on internationally recognised cloud platforms (such as Google Cloud). We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy or as required by applicable law. Contact form submissions are typically retained for 3 years. Client project data is retained for 5 years following project completion or as agreed contractually.

While we take data security seriously, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

Under applicable Indian law (including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023) and, where applicable, international frameworks, you have the right to:

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@clickdecoded.com with the subject line "Data Privacy Request." We will respond within 30 days.

📋 How It Works

Our Process — Step by Step

Transparent process. You know what we're doing at every stage.

01
Store Planning & Catalogue Mapping
We understand your product range, business model (B2C/B2B/D2C), shipping areas, and payment preferences. We plan the store architecture — categories, variants, filters — before writing a line of code.
02
Design & UX
We design mobile-first wireframes and high-fidelity mockups for the homepage, category pages, product pages, and checkout. Conversion-focused layouts — not just pretty designs.
03
Development & Integration
We build the store, integrate your chosen payment gateway (Razorpay/PayU), shipping provider (Shiprocket/Delhivery), and GST invoicing. We set up the admin panel so your team can manage products, orders, and inventory.
04
Product Upload & Content Setup
We either upload your initial product catalogue or train your team to do it. We configure product attributes, variants, pricing, and stock levels.
05
Testing & Launch
Full testing: payment flow, order emails, mobile experience, load speed, and checkout conversion. We fix everything before go-live. Then we launch, submit to Google, and set up Analytics e-commerce tracking.

Our Process

First Article Live
in Week One.

A structured onboarding that understands your brand, maps your keyword opportunities, and has your first optimised article published before the end of Week 1.

01

Brand & Competitor Audit

We audit your existing content, map your competitor's top-ranking articles, and identify keyword gaps — the topics they rank for that you don't. This shapes your entire content strategy and prioritises quick-win opportunities.

02

Keyword Strategy

Monthly keyword research identifying 20–40 target topics mapped by search volume, keyword difficulty, and purchase intent. Articles prioritised so you're targeting achievable rankings first — building domain authority before tackling competitive head terms.

03

Write, Optimise & Review

Articles written, SEO-optimised, and sent for your review before publishing. One round of revisions included per article — we incorporate your feedback and product knowledge to ensure content is accurate and on-brand before going live.

04

Publish & Track

Articles published to your CMS (WordPress, Webflow, Shopify, etc.) with proper formatting, images, and schema markup. Ranking positions tracked in GSC and Ahrefs — monthly reports show exactly which keywords are moving and by how much.
